Welcome to Lazy P Bar Ranch and Rescue. We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, incorporated in 2014 with the goal of aiding and assisting equines who need to feel loved and properly cared for in better homes. It is run by Pat Potter McAndrews and her husband Butch, with the assistance of dedicated board members. The name "Lazy P Bar" was selected because it is the brand from our family since the 1800s.

Pat was introduced to horses as a child growing up in Colorado, and has never lost her passion for these wonderful animals. She has ridden, raced, bred, trained, and camped with horses all her life.

The Ranch is also associated with public service porganizations that work in emergencies and disasters trying to help equines. Currently we are working on raising money to buy expensive but necessary equipment for trained professionals to use in animal rescue.

Tax deductible donations are truly appreciated to aid in these worthy causes. Please click the "Donate" button above to see the ways in which you can contribute financially to Lazy P Bar Ranch and Rescue.
